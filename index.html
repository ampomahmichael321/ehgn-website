ABOUT US

Our History

Eleventh Hour Generation Network (EHGN) is non-denominational Christian fellowship envisioned to stir up the Revival fire in believers and prepare them for the coming of Jesus. The ministry has an outreach arm known as The Jesus Project which mainly aims at inter-school evangelical invasions, career seminars, health screening and other humanitarian activities

Ps. Christopher Ampomah

Founder and Lead Pastor, Eleventh Hour Generation Network

Our Mission

Eleventh Hour Generation Network is a movement called to awaken, equip, and release a generation for the work of the Kingdom in these critical last days. We are committed to hearing the voice of God, declaring His heart for this generation, and empowering believers to walk in the fullness of their God-given destiny.

Our Vision

Our vision is to raise up a generation that will boldly proclaim God’s message of hope, restoration, and urgency to the world. We seek to cultivate a network of leaders who are equipped to discern the times and carry God’s transformative power into every sphere of society ; including professions, leadership, and politics.
