ABOUT US

Our History

Founded in 2005, Eleventh Hour Generation Network (EHGN) began as a small fellowship of believers passionate about preparing for the return of Jesus Christ. Over the years, we have grown into a global ministry with a presence in over 30 countries, impacting lives and communities through our various programs and outreaches.

Ps. Christopher Ampomah

Founder and Lead Pastor, Eleventh Hour Generation Network

Our Mission

To equip and empower believers worldwide to live purpose-driven lives, spreading the message of hope, love, and salvation through Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

To see a generation of believers fully prepared for the return of Jesus Christ, impacting their communities and the world with the transformative power of the Gospel.
